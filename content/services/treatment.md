Fresh Start Recovery Programme offers specialist addiction treatment (detox and relapse prevention) on Wednesdays and Saturdays (excluding long weekends). Treatments for sedative dependence are typically started on Fridays.
Addiction Medicine Specialist & Medical Director 

george_headshotMBBS, FRCOG, FRACOG, DDU, FAChAM, Cit.WA

Dr O'Neil is a Fellow of the Australian Chapter of Addiction Medicine within the Adult Medicine Division of the Royal Australian College of Physicians. This chapter provides specialist training and represents the interests of specialists in the field of addiction medicine.

Dr O'Neil has pioneered the use of opiate antagonists in Australia for opiate addiction and since 1996 he has concentrated his main efforts on running an addiction treatment clinic in Subiaco, now part of Fresh Start Recovery Programme.

Fresh Start Recovery Programme is committed to developing and improving medical procedures in the field of addiction medicine. In conjunction with The University of Western Australia and Go Medical Industries, Fresh Start is constantly researching how to improve treatments for addictions. Current projects include research into opiate addiction, amphetamine-type stimulants, alcohol and benzodiazepine dependence.
